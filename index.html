ServiceSPOTTER

servicespotter.com


Currently there are thousands of individuals experiencing homelessness in Austin. With tough economic conditions due to COVID, the low-income community has increased significantly in last few years. In Summer 2020, I conducted a survey of the local low-income and homeless community, which reflected a critical need and lack of access to various service providers.

ServiceSPOTTER is free iOS App available on Apple App store. It was independently developed by me as an interactive tool to serve homeless and Low-income communities to access various service providers to find and connect to get their basic needs of food, clothing, shelter, and health care, besides helplines. It also allows the volunteers to be able to assist the service providers (critical shortage).
